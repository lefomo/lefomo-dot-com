Swallow Me Whole
LE FOMO is set to release our first studio produced full length, Swallow Me Whole, in 2020. A maelstrom of powerful vocals, swooning lyrics, driving bass lines, catchy guitar riffs, shimmering synth, and frenetic beats, Swallow Me Whole was recorded and mixed by Maryam Qudus at Tiny Telephone and Best House Studios, and is currently being mastered by Piper Payne at Infrasonic Studios.
